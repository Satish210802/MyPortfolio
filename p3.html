YOGA POSE DETECTION USING MACHINE LEARNING

Key skills & Tools:HTML | CSS | JAVASCRIPT | PYTHON | ML | CV

Yoga, known for its holistic benefits encompassing physical fitness and mental well-being, gained popularity as a means to manage stress and maintain health during these challenging times. In the COVID-19 pandemic, the absence of in-person guidance and feedback posed a challenge for practitioners, especially those new to yoga. It has significantly altered how individuals engage in physical and mental well-being activities. To address this gap, technology emerged as a solution to enhance the online yoga experience. Integrating Online Yoga Pose Detection Using AI not only brings the advantages of real-time feedback and personalized guidance but also helps individuals maintain a sense of connection with their practice, even when isolated at home.
