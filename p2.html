ANAND-HOSPITAL MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Key skills & Tools:HTML | CSS | JAVASCRIPT | PHP | BOOTSTRAP

Many people faced challenges during the pandemic, such as difficulties with offline appointment registration and physical handling of patient records due to social distancing and reduced physical contact. To address these issues, we introduced a platform aimed at enhancing communication among various stakeholders in healthcare settings. On this platform, different stakeholders, including doctors, patients, and administrative staff, can access specific features such as the dashboard, managing doctor or patient records, prescriptions, and appointment registration. Certain functionalities are restricted to administrators to ensure data security and privacy. Notably, our system efficiently handles emergency cases, ensuring prompt responses and improved patient care. This integrated approach not only facilitates seamless communication but also improves efficiency, productivity, and patient care.
